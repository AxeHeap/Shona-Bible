\b	Nehemiya
\c	1
\s	Munyengetero waNehemiya
\p
\v	1	MashokoaNehemiya mwanakomana waHakariya. Zvino zvakaitika mumwedzi weKisirevhi, mugore remakumi maviri, ini pandaiva ndiri paShushani muzinda,
\v	2	kutiHanani, umwe wehama dzangu, wakasvika iye nevarume vaibva kwaJudha; zvino ndakavabvunza maererano nevaJudha vaiva vapukunyuka vakasara pautapwa, uye maererano neJerusarema.
\v	3	Zvinovakati kwandiri: Vasiiwa, vakasarapo pakutapwa padunhu, vari pakutambudzika kukuru nepakushorwa; uye rusvingo rweJerusaremawo rwakoromoka, nemasuwo aro apiswa nemoto.
\p
\v	4	Zvinozvakaitika pandanzwa mashoko awa, kuti ndakagara pasi ndokuchema, ndokurira  kwemazuva; uye ndaitsanya, nekunyengetera pamberi paMwari wekumatenga.
\v	5	Zvinondakati: Haiwa Jehovha Mwari wekumatenga, Mwari mukuru uye anotyisa, anochengetedza sungano netsitsi kune vanomuda uye vachichengeta mirairo yake.
\v	6	Nzeveyenyu ikozvino ngaiteerere, nemeso enyu asvinure, kuti munzwe munyengetero wemuranda wenyu, wandinonyengetera pamberi penyu nhasi, masikati neusiku pamusoro pevana vaIsraeri varanda venyu, uye ndichireurura nekuda kwezvivi zvevana vaIsraeri zvatakakutadzirai. Hongu, ini neveimba yababa vangu takatadza.
\v	7	Takakuitiraizvakaipa kwazvo, uye hatina kuchengeta mirairo nezvimiso nemitongo zvamakaraira muranda wenyu Mozisi.
\v	8	Rangariraihenyu shoko ramakaraira muranda wenyu Mozisi muchiti:  Kana imwi mukadarika, ini ndichakuparadzirai pakati pemarudzi.
\v	9	Kanaimwi mukadzokera kwandiri, mukachengeta mirairo yangu nekuiita, kana vakarashwa venyu vari kumacheto ekumatenga, ndichavaunganidza kubva ipapo, uye ndichavaunza panzvimbo yandakasarudza kugarisa zita rangupo.
\v	10	Zvino ava varanda venyu uye vanhu venyu, vamakadzikinura nesimba renyu guru, uye neruoko rwenyu rwune simba.
\v	11	Haiwa, Ishe, nzeve yenyu ngaiteerere ikozvino kumunyengetero wemuranda wenyu nekumunyengetero wevaranda venyu vanoshuva kutya zita renyu; mugobudiririsa henyu muranda wenyu nhasi, mumupe ngoni pamberi pemunhu uyu.
\p Zvino ini ndaiva ndiri mudiri wamambo.
\c	10
\s	Vanhu vanoita sungano naMwari
\p
\v	1	Zvinopakudhinda paiva naNehemiya mutongi, mwanakomana waHakariya, naZedhekia,
\v	2	Seraya,Azariya, Jeremiya,
\v	3	Pashuri,Amariya, Marikiya,
\v	4	Hatushi,Shebhaniya, Maruki,
\v	5	Harimi,Meremoti, Obhadhiya,
\v	6	Dhaniyeri,Ginetoni, Bharuki,
\v	7	Meshurami,Abhiya, Mijamini,
\v	8	Maaziya,Bhirigai, Shemaya. Ava vaiva vapristi.
\v	9	NevaRevhi:Vose Jeshuwa mwanakomana waAzaniya, Bhinui, wevanakomana vaHenadhadhi, Kadhimieri.
\v	10	Nehama dzavo: Shebhaniya, Hodhiya, Kerita, Peraya, Hanani,
\v	11	Mika, Rehobhi, Hashabhiya,
\v	12	Zakuri, Sherebhiya, Shebhaniya,
\v	13	Hodhiya, Bhani, Bheninu.
\v	14	Misoro yevanhu: Paroshi, Pahati-Moabhi, Erami, Zatu, Bhani,
\v	15	Bhuni, Azigadhi, Bhebhayi,
\v	16	Adhoniya, Bhigivhayi, Adhini,
\v	17	Ateri, Hezekia, Azuri,
\v	18	Hodhiya, Hashumi, Bhezai,
\v	19	Harifi, Anatoti, Nebhai,
\v	20	Magipiashi, Meshurami, Heziri,
\v	21	Meshezabheri, Zadhoki, Jadhua,
\v	22	Peratiya, Hanani, Anaya,
\v	23	Hosheya, Hananiya, Hashubhi,
\v	24	Haroheshi, Piriha, Shobheki,
\v	25	Rehumi, Hashabhina, Maaseya,
\v	26	naAhiya, Hanani, Anani,
\v	27	Maruki, Harimi, Bhaana.
\p
\v	28	Zvino vakasara pavanhu, vapristi, vaRevhi, varindi vemikova, vaimbi, VaNetinimi, nevose vaiva vazvitsaurira murairo waMwari kubva pavanhu venyika idzo, vakadzi vavo, vanakomana vavo, nevakunda vavo, umwe neumwe waiziva wainzwisisa,
\v	29	vakabatirira kuhama dzavo, vakuru vavo, ndokupinda muchakatukwa nepamhiko, kuti vafambe mumurairo waMwari wakapiwa neruoko rwaMozisi muranda waMwari, nekuchengetedza nekuita mirairo yose yaJehovha Ishe wedu, nemitongo yake nezvimiso zvake;
\v	30	uye kuti hatingapi vakunda vedu kuvanhu venyika iyo, uye hatingatoreri vanakomana vedu vakunda vavo.
\v	31	Kana vanhu venyika vakauya nenhumbi kana zviyo zvipi nezvipi nezuva resabata kuzotengesa, hatina kutenga kwavari nesabata kana nezuva dzvene. Uye ticharega gore rechinomwe, nechikwereti cheruoko rwumwe nerwumwe.
\v	32	Uye tichamisa patiri mirairo, kuisa patiri chetatu cheshekeri pagore, chive chebasa reimba yaMwari wedu,
\v	33	chechingwa chekuratidzira, nechechipo chechidyiwa chemazuva ose, nechechipo chinopiswa chemazuva ose, chemasabata, chekugara kwemwedzi, chemitambo yakatarwa, nechezvitsvene, nechezvipo zvechivi, kuitira Israeri chiyananiso, nechebasa rose reimba yaMwari wedu.
\v	34	Zvino isu, vapristi, vaRevhi, nevanhu, takakanda mijenya pamusoro pechipo chehuni, kuuya na cho muimba yaMwari wedu, zvichienderana neimba yemadzibaba edu, panguva dzakatarwa gore negore, kupisa paaritari yaJehovha Mwari wedu, sezvakanyorwa pamurairo;
\v	35	nekuuya nezvibereko zvekutanga zvevhu redu nezvibereko zvekutanga zvemichero yose yemiti yose gore negore kuimba yaJehovha;
\v	36	nematangwe evanakomana vedu neezvipfuwo zvedu, sezvakanyorwa mumurairo, nematangwe emombe dzedu nemakwai edu, kuuisa kuimba yaMwari wedu, kuvapristi vanoshumira muimba yaMwari wedu;
\v	37	uye zvekutanga zveupfu hwedu, nezvipo zvedu, nezvibereko zvemiti yose, zvewaini itsva uye zvemafuta, tiuye nazvo kumupristi mumakamuri eimba yaMwari wedu, nezvegumi zvevhu redu kuvaRevhi, kuti vaRevhi ivavo vagamuchire chegumi kumaguta ose ebasa redu.
\v	38	Zvino mupristi mwanakomana waAroni achava nevaRevhi, vaRevhi pavachatora zvegumi. Zvino vaRevhi vachakwidza chegumi chechegumi kuimba yaMwari wedu kumakamuri eimba yezvinokosha.
\v	39	Nokuti vana vaIsraeri nevana vaRevhi vachauya nechipo chezviyo, chewaini itsva, nemafuta, kumakamuri, nokuti imo mune midziyo yeimba tsvene, nevapristi vanoshanda, nevarindi vemikova, nevaimbi. Zvino hatingasii imba yaMwari wedu.
\c	11
\s	Vanhu vanoratidzwa pavangagara
\p
\v	1	Zvinovatongi vevanhu vakagara paJerusarema; vakasara pavanhu ndokukanda mijenya, kuunza umwe pagumi kuti agare muJerusarema guta dzvene, uye zvikamu zvipfumbamwe kune  amwe maguta.
\v	2	Zvinovanhu vakaropafadza varume vose vakazvipira nechido kunogara muJerusarema.
\p
\v	3	Zvinoiyi ndiyo misoro yedunhu yakagara muJerusarema; asi mumaguta aJudha makagara umwe neumwe panzvimbo yake pamaguta avo, Israeri, vapristi, nevaRevhi, nevaNetinimi, nevana vevaranda vaSoromoni.
\v	4	ZvinopaJerusarema pakagara  vamwe vevana vaJudha, nevevana vaBhenjamini. Pavana vaJudha: Ataya mwanakomana waUziya mwanakomana waZekariya mwanakomana waAmariya mwanakomana waShefatiya mwanakomana waMahararieri wevana vaPerezi;
\v	5	naMaaseyamwanakomana waBharuki mwanakomana waKori-Hoze mwanakomana waHazaya mwanakomana waAdhaya mwanakomana waJoyaribhi mwanakomana waZekariya mwanakomana waShironi.
\v	6	Vanakomanavose vaPerezi vakagara paJerusarema vaiva mazana mana nemakumi matanhatu nevasere, varume veumhare.
\v	7	Zvinoava vanakomana vaBhenjamini: Saru mwanakomana waMeshurami mwanakomana waJoedhi mwanakomana waPedhaya mwanakomana waKoraya mwanakomana waMaaseya mwanakomana waItieri mwanakomana waJeshaya.
\v	8	Zvinoshure kwake Gabhai, Sarai, mazana mapfumbamwe nemakumi maviri nevasere.
\v	9	NaJowerimwanakomana waZikiri waiva mutariri pamusoro pavo; naJudha mwanakomana waHasenuwa waiva wepiri pamusoro peguta.
\v	10	Pavapristi: Jedhaya mwanakomana waJoyaribhi, Jakini,
\v	11	Seraya mwanakomana waHirikiya mwanakomana waMeshurami mwanakomana waZadhoki mwanakomana waMerayoti mwanakomana waAhitubhi, mutungamiriri weimba yaMwari.
\v	12	Nehama dzavo dzaiita basa reimba iyo, mazana masere nemakumi maviri nevaviri. NaAdhaya mwanakomana waJerohami mwanakomana waPeraira mwanakomana waAmizi mwanakomana waZekariya mwanakomana waPashuri mwanakomana waMarikiya.
\v	13	Nehama dzake, misoro yemadzibaba, mazana maviri nemakumi mana nevaviri. NaAmashisayi mwanakomana waAzareeri mwanakomana waAzai mwanakomana waMeshiremoti mwanakomana waImeri.
\v	14	Nehama dzavo, varume vane simba veumhare, zana nemakumi maviri nevasere. Uye mutariri pamusoro pavo waiva Zabhidhieri mwanakomana wavakuru.
\v	15	Uye pavaRevhi: Shemaya mwanakomana waHashubhi mwanakomana waAzirikamu mwanakomana waHashabhiya mwanakomana waBhuni.
\v	16	NaShabhetai naJozabhadhi vari vemisoro yevaRevhi pamusoro pebasa rekunze reimba yaMwari.
\v	17	NaMataniya mwanakomana waMika mwanakomana waZabhidhi mwanakomana waAsafi, mukuru wekutanga kuvonga pamunyengetero; naBhakibhukiya, wepiri pakati pehama dzake; naAbhidha, mwanakomana waShamua, mwanakomana waGarari, mwanakomana waJedhutuni.
\v	18	VaRevhi vose paguta dzvene vaiva mazana maviri nemakumi masere nevana.
\v	19	Uye varindi vemikova, Akubhi, Tarimoni, nehama dzavo, vairinda pamikova, vaiva zana nemakumi manomwe nevaviri.
\p
\v	20	Nevasiiwa vaIsraeri, vapristi, vaRevhi, vaiva pamaguta ose aJudha; umwe neumwe panhaka yake.
\v	21	Asi VaNetinimi vakagara paOferi; uye Ziha naGishipa vaiva pamusoro pevaNetinimi.
\v	22	Mutaririwo wevaRevhi paJerusarema waiva Uzi mwanakomana waBhani mwanakomana waHashabhiya mwanakomana waMataniya mwanakomana waMika. Pavanakomana vaAsafi, vaimbi vaiva pamusoro pebasa reimba yaMwari.
\v	23	Nokuti waiva murairo wamambo maererano navo, murau wakasimba kuvaimbi, nyaya yezuva nezuva rayo.
\v	24	NaPetahiya mwanakomana waMeshazabheeri, wevana vaZera mwanakomana waJudha, waiva paruoko rwamambo panyaya yose yevanhu.
\v	25	Nepamisha nekuruwa rwayo, pavana vaJudha vakagara paKiriyati-Aribha nemisha yaro, nepaDhibhoni nemisha yaro, nepaJekabhizeeri nemisha yaro,
\v	26	nepaJeshuwa, nepaMoradha, nepaBheti-Pereti,
\v	27	nepaHazari-Shuari, nepaBheeri-Shebha nemisha yaro,
\v	28	nepaZikiragi, nepaMekona, nepamisha yaro,
\v	29	nepaEni-Rimoni, nepaZora, nepaJarimuti,
\v	30	Zanowa, Adhurami, nemisha yawo, Rakishi neruwa rwaro, Azeka nemisha yaro. Naizvozvo vakavaka misasa kubva paBheeri-Shebha kusvika pamupata weHinomi.
\v	31	Nevana vaBhenjamini kubva paGebha, Mikimashi, neAiya, neBheteri nemisha yawo,
\v	32	Anatoti, Nobhi, Ananiya,
\v	33	Hazori, Rama, Gitaimi,
\v	34	Hadhidhi, Zebhoimi, Nebharati,
\v	35	Rodhi, neOno, mupata wedzimhizha.
\v	36	NepavaRevhi mapoka aJudha, neaBhenjamini.
\c	12
\s	Vapristi nevaRevhi
\p
\v	1	Zvinoava vapristi nevaRevhi vakakwira naZerubhabheri mwanakomana waShearitieri naJeshuwa: Seraya, Jeremiya, Ezira,
\v	2	Amariya,Maruki, Hatushi,
\v	3	Shekaniya,Rehumi, Meremoti,
\v	4	Idho,Ginetoi, Abhiya,
\v	5	Mijamini,Maadhiya, Bhiriga,
\v	6	Shemaya,naJoyaribhi, Jedhaya,
\v	7	Saru,Amoki, Hirikiya, Jedhaya. Ava misoro yevapristi neyehama dzavo pamazuva aJeshuwa.
\v	8	UyevaRevhi: Jeshuwa, Bhinui, Kadhimieri, Sherebhia, Judha, Mataniya, vaiva pamusoro pekuvonga, iye nehama dzake.
\v	9	NaBhakibhukiya,naUni, hama dzavo, vakatarisana navo pazvirindo.
\p
\v	10	Zvino Jeshuwa wakabereka Joyakimu; Joyakimu ndokubereka Eriashibhi; Eriashibhi ndo kubereka Joyadha;
\v	11	Joyadha ndokubereka Jonatani; Jonatani ndokubereka Jadhuwa.
\v	12	Zvino pamazuva aJoyakimu vapristi, misoro yemadzibaba, vaiva: WekwaSeraya, Meraya; wekwaJeremiya, Hananiya;
\v	13	wekwaEzira, Meshurami; wekwaAmariya, Jehohanani;
\v	14	wekwaMaruki, Jonatani; wekwaShebhaniya, Josefa;
\v	15	wekwaHarimi, Adhina; wekwaMerayoti, Herikai;
\v	16	wekwaIdho, Zekariya; wekaGinetoni, Meshurami;
\v	17	wekwaAbhiya, Zikiri; wekwaMiniyamini, wekwaMoadhiya, Piritai;
\v	18	wekwaBhiriga, Shamuwa; wekwaShemaya, Jehonatani;
\v	19	uye wekwaJoyaribhi, Matenai; wekwaJedhaya, Uzi;
\v	20	wekwaSarai, Karai; wekwaAmoki, Ebheri;
\v	21	wekwaHirikiya, Hashabhiya; wekwaJedhaya, Netaneri.
\p
\v	22	VaRevhi pamazuva aEriashibhi, Joyadha, naJohwanani, naJadhuwa, vakanyorwa vakava misoro yemadzibaba, nevapristi kusvika pava paushe hwaDhariyasi muPerisiya.
\v	23	Vanakomana vaRevhi, misoro yemadzibaba, vakanyorwa pabhuku reMakoronike kusvika pamazuva aJohwanani mwanakomana waEriashibhi.
\v	24	Nemisoro yevaRevhi: Hashabhiya, Sherebhiya, naJeshuwa mwanakomana waKadhimieri, nehama dzavo dzakatarisana navo, kurumbidza, kuvonga, zvichienderana nemurairo waDhavhidhi munhu waMwari, chirindo chakatarisana nechirindo.
\v	25	Mataniya, naBhakibhukiya, Obhadhiya, Meshurami, Tarimoni, Akubhi vaiva varindi vemikova, vachirinda chirindo pamatura emasuwo.
\v	26	Ava vaiva ko pamazuva aJoyakimu mwanakomana waJeshuwa mwanakomana waJozadhaki, nepamazuva aNehemiya mutongi, naEzira mupristi munyori.
\s	Rusvingo rweJerusarema runotsaurirwa Jehovha
\p
\v	27	Zvino pakutsaura rusvingo rweJerusarema vakatsvaka vaRevhi kubva panzvimbo dzavo dzose kuuya navo kuJerusarema, kuzoita kutsaura nemufaro, uye nekuvonga uye nekuimba, makandira, mitengeranwa, uye nembira.
\v	28	Zvino vanakomana vevaimbi vakaungana, zvose kubva kumapani akapoteredza Jerusarema nekubva kumisha yevaNetofati,
\v	29	nekubva paBheti-Girigari, uye kubva pamaruwa eGebha neAzimavheti, nokuti vaimbi vaiva vazvivakira misha yakapoteredza Jerusarema.
\v	30	Zvino vapristi nevaRevhi vakazvichenesa, ndokuchenesa vanhu, nemasuwo, nerusvingo.
\p
\v	31	Zvino ndakakwidza misoro yaJudha pamusoro perusvingo, ndokugadza mapoka makuru maviri kuvonga nemidungwe, kurudyi pamusoro perusvingo kusvika kusuwo remupfudze.
\v	32	Zvino shure kwavo kwakafamba Hoshaya, nehafu yemisoro yaJudha,
\v	33	naAzariya, Ezira, naMeshurami,
\v	34	Judha, naBhenjamini, naShemaya, naJeremiya.
\v	35	Nepavanakomana vevapristi vane hwamanda: Zekariya mwanakomana waJonatani mwanakomana waShemaya mwanakomana waMataniya mwanakomana waMikaya mwanakomana waZakuri mwanakomana waAsafi.
\v	36	Nehama dzake: Shemaya, naAzareri, Mirarai, Girarai, Maai, Netaneri, naJudha, Hanani, vane midziyo yekuimbisa yaDhavhidhi munhu waMwari, uye Ezira munyori waiva pamberi pavo.
\v	37	Uye nepasuwo repachitubu nepakatarisana navo vakakwira nematanho eguta raDhavhidhi, pamukwidza werusvingo, pamusoro peimba yaDhavhidhi, kusvikawo pasuwo remvura kumabvazuva.
\v	38	Neboka repiri rekuvonga rakafamba rakatarisana  navo, neni ndichiritevera, uye hafu yevanhu pamusoro perusvingo, kubva pamusoro peshongwe yemavira emoto, kusvika parusvingo rwakapamhama,
\v	39	uye kubva pamusoro pesuwo raEfuremu, nepamusoro pesuwo rekare, nepamusoro pesuwo rehove, neshongwe yaHananeri, neshongwe yeMeya, kusvika pasuwo remakwai, ndokumira pasuwo revarindi.
\v	40	Mapoka ari maviri ekuvonga ndokumira muimba yaMwari, neni nehafu yevatariri ineni;
\v	41	nevapristi Eriakimu, Maaseya, Miniyamini, Mikaya, Erioenai, Zekariya, Hananiya, vane hwamanda;
\v	42	naMaaseya, naShemaya, naEriazari, naUzi, naJehohanani, naMarikiya, naErami, naEzeri. Zvino vaimbi vakaimbisa, vana Jezirahiya mutariri.
\v	43	Zvino nezuva iro vakabaira zvibairo zvikuru ndokufara; nokuti Mwari waiva avafadza nemufaro mukuru; nevakadziwo nevana vakafara, kusvika mufaro weJerusarema wanzwikwa kure.
\p
\v	44	Zvino nezuva iro vamwe vakagadzwa pamusoro pemakamuri ezvinokosha, zvezvipo, zvezvezvibereko zvekutanga, uye zvezvegumi, kuunganidza maari zvinobva muminda yemaguta migove yemurairo yevapristi neyevaRevhi, nokuti Judha wakafarira vapristi nevaRevhi vaiva vamire.
\v	45	Zvino vakachengeta faniro yaMwari wavo, nefaniro yekuchenesa, vose vaimbi nevarindi vemikova zvichienderana nemurairo waDhavhidhi  naSoromoni mwanakomana wake.
\v	46	Nokuti pamazuva aDhavhidhi naAsafi vevekare, kwaiva nemisoro yevaimbi uye rwiyo rwekurumbidza nekuvonga kuna Mwari.
\v	47	Zvino Israeri wose pamazuva aZerubhabheri nepamazuva aNehemiya vaipa migove yevaimbi nevarindi vemikova, nyaya yezuva pazuva rayo; ndokutsaurira vaRevhi, uye vaRevhi vaitsaurira vanakomana vaAroni.
\c	13
\s	Zvimwe zvakagadzirwa naNehemiya
\p
\v	1	Nezuvairo kwakaverengwa mubhuku raMozisi munzeve dzevanhu; zvino mariri makawanikwa makanyorwa kuti vaAmoni nevaMoabhu havafaniri kupinda muungano yaMwari nekusingaperi;
\v	2	nokutihavana kuchingura vana vaIsraeri nezvekudya uye nemvura, asi vakavahaira Bharamu kuti avatuke; asi Mwari wedu wakashandura chituko chikava ropafadzo.
\v	3	Zvinozvakaitika pavakanzwa murairo kuti vakatsaura kubva kuna Israeri musanganiswa wose wendudzi.
\p
\v	4	Zvinopamberi peizvi Eriashibhi mupristi waiva aiswa  kuva pamusoro pemakamuri eimba yaMwari wedu, achishamwaridzana naTobhiya.
\v	5	Zvinowaiva amugadzirira kamuri huru pavaimbochengetera chipo chechidyiwa, zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira, nemidziyo, nezvegumi zvezviyo, waini itsva, nemafuta, zvaiva zvarairwa vaRevhi, nevaimbi, nevarindi vemikova, nechipo chevapristi.
\v	6	Asipane izvi zvose ndaiva ndisipo paJerusarema; nokuti pagore remakumi matatu nemaviri raAritakiserikisi mambo weBhabhironi, ndakauya kuna mambo; uye pakupera kwemazuva ndakakumbira tendero kuna mambo.
\v	7	NdokusvikakuJerusarema, ndokunzwisisa zvechakaipa Eriashibhi chaakaitira Tobhiya pakumuitira kamuri muzvivanze zveimba yaMwari.
\v	8	Zvinozvakava zvakaipa zvikuru kwandiri; naizvozvo ndakakandira panze nhumbi dzose dzeimba yaTobhiya kunze kwekamuri.
\v	9	Zvinondakaraira, uye vakanatsa makamuri; ini ndokudzoserapo midziyo yeimba yaMwari pamwe nechipo chechidyiwa nezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\p
\v	10	Zvino ndakaziva kuti migove yevaRevhi yaiva isina kupiwa; naizvozvo vaRevhi nevaimbi vaiita basa vaiva vatizira umwe neumwe kumunda wake.
\v	11	Zvino ndakapikisana nevatongi ndokuti: Sei imba yaMwari yasiiwa? Zvino ndakavaunganidza, ndokuvamisa panzvimbo yavo.
\v	12	Zvino Judha wose wakauya nechegumi chezviyo nechewaini itsva nechemafuta kumatura.
\v	13	Zvino ndakaisa vachengeti vematura pamusoro pematura, Sheremiya mupristi, naZadhoki munyori, naPedhaya pavaRevhi; uye parutivi rwavo Hanani mwanakomana waZakuri, mwanakomana waMataniya; nokuti vaiverengwa vakatendeka; uye zvaiva pavari kugovera kuhama dzavo.
\v	14	Ndirangarirei, Mwari wangu, maererano neichi, uye musadzima zviito zvangu zvakanaka zvandakaitira imba yaMwari wangu neshumiro dzayo.
\p
\v	15	Pamazuva iwawo ndakaona kwaJudha vaitsika chisviniro chewaini nesabata, uye vachipinza zvisote nekutakudza mbongoro, newainiwo, mazambiringa, nemaonde, nemitoro yemarudzi ose, zvavakapinza muJerusarema nezuva resabata. Zvino ndakapupura pazuva ravaitengesa zvekudya.
\v	16	Paiva pagerewo mairi VaTire, vaiuya nehove, nenhumbi dzose, ndokutengesa nesabata kuvana vaJudha nepaJerusarema.
\v	17	Zvino ndakapikisana nevakuru vaJudha, ndokuti kwavari: Chii chinhu ichi chakaipa chamunoita, muchisvibisa zuva resabata?
\v	18	Madzibaba enyu haana kuita saizvozvo here, naMwari wedu akaunza pamusoro pedu zvakaipa zvose izvi nepamusoro pemusha uyu? Zvakadaro munowedzera kutsamwa pamusoro paIsraeri nekusvibisa sabata.
\v	19	Zvino zvakaitika kuti masuwo eJerusarema paakatanga kusviba sabata risati rasvika, ndakaraira kuti mikova ipfigwe, ndokuraira kuti irege kuzarurwa kusvika shure kwesabata; uye kubva pavaranda vangu ndakaisa pamasuwo, kusapinda mutoro pazuva resabata.
\v	20	Naizvozvo vashambadziri nevatengesi venhumbi dzose vakarara kunze kweJerusarema kamwe kana kaviri.
\v	21	Zvino ndakavapupurira ndokuti kwavari: Munorarirei pakatarisana nerusvingo? Kana mukapamhidza, ndichabudisira ruoko pamusoro penyu. Kubva panguva iyo havana kuzouya nesabata.
\v	22	Zvino ndakaraira vaRevhi kuti vazvinatse uye kuti vauye  kuzorinda masuwo, kuti vaite dzvene zuva resabata. Mundirangarire neizvi, Mwari wangu, mundinzwire tsitsi nekuwanda kwenyasha dzenyu.
\p
\v	23	Mumazuva iwawo ndakaonawo vaJudha vaiva vawana vakadzi VaAshidhodhikadzi, vaAmonikadzi, vaMoabhukadzi.
\v	24	Zvino vana vavo, hafu vakataura chiAshidhodhi, uye vasingagoni kutaura chiJudha, asi nerurimi rwerudzi nerudzi.
\v	25	Zvino ndakapikisana navo, ndokuvatuka, ndokurova vamwe vavo, ndokudzvura vhudzi ravo, ndokuvapikisa naMwari  ndichiti: Hamungapi vakunda venyu kuvanakomana vavo, uye hamungatoreri vanakomana venyu kubva kuvakunda vavo, kana imwi pachenyu.
\v	26	Soromoni mambo waIsraeri haana kutadza nezvinhu izvi here? Zvakadaro pakati pendudzi zhinji paiva pasina mambo wakaita saiye, naye waiva mudikanwa waMwari wake, Mwari ndokumuita mambo pamusoro paIsraeri wose; naiye vakadzi vatorwa vakamuita kuti atadze.
\v	27	Zvino tingakuteererai kuita chinhu ichi chakaipa chikuru here, kutadzira Mwari wedu nekuwana vakadzi vatorwa?
\v	28	Zvino  umwe wevanakomana vaJoyadha mwanakomana waEriashibhi mupristi mukuru waiva mukwasha waSanibharati muHoroni; saka ndakamudzinga abve kwandiri.
\v	29	Varangarirei, Mwari wangu, nokuti vakasvibisa upristi nesungano yeupristi neyevaRevhi.
\v	30	Saizvozvo ndakavanatsa pavatorwa vose, ndokumisa faniro dzevapristi nevaRevhi, umwe neumwe pabasa rake;
\v	31	nepazvipo zvehuni panguva dzakatarwa, nepazvibereko zvekutanga. Ndirangarirei, Mwari wangu, pazvakanaka.
\c	2
\s	Aritakiserikisi anotendera Nehemiya kunovaka Jerusarema
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika mumwedzi weNisani, mugore remakumi maviri ramambo Aritakiserikisi, waini iri pamberi pake. Zvino ndakasimudza waini, ndokupa kuna mambo. Zvino ndaiva ndisati ndambosuwa pamberi pake.
\v	2	Mambondokuti kwandiri: Chiso chako chakasuruvara nei iwe zvausingarwari? Hachisi chinhu kunze kwekusuwa kwemoyo. Zvino ndakatya zvikuru kwazvo.
\v	3	Zvinondakati kuna mambo: Mambo ngaararame nekusingaperi! Chiso changu chingaregerei kusuwa, kana guta muzinda wemakuva emadzibaba angu riri dongo, nemasuwo aro apedzwa nemoto?
\v	4	Mambondokuti kwandiri: Ikozvino unotsvakei? Zvino ndakanyengetera kuna Mwari wekumatenga.
\v	5	Zvinondakati kuna mambo: Kana zvakanaka kuna mambo, uye kana muranda wenyu awana nyasha pamberi penyu, kuti munditume kwaJudha, kuguta remakuva emadzibaba angu, zvino ndichanorivaka.
\v	6	Mambondokuti kwandiri (vahosiwo vaiva vagere naye): Rwendo rwako ruchava kusvika rinhi? Uchadzoka rinhi? Zvino mambo akatenda kundituma. Uye ndikamutarira nguva.
\v	7	Ndakatiwokuna mambo: Kana zvichifadza mambo, tsamba ngadzipiwe kwandiri kuenda kuvatongi mhiri kwerwizi, kuti vandipfuudze kusvika ndasvika kwaJudha;
\v	8	netsambakuna Asafi muchengeti wesango riri ramambo, kuti ape kwandiri matanda ekuita mapuranga emasuwo emuzinda waiva nechepamba, neerusvingo rweguta, neeimba yandichapinda mairi. Mambo ndokundipa zvichienderana neruoko rwakanaka rwaMwari wangu pamusoro pangu.
\p
\v	9	Zvinondakasvika kuvatongi mhiri kwerwizi, ndokuvapa tsamba dzamambo. Zvino mambo waiva atuma pamwe neni vatungamiriri vehondo nevatasvi vemabhiza.
\v	10	Sanibharati muHoroni naTobhiya muranda muAmoni pavakanzwa, vakadedera zvakaipa kwazvo kuti kwakasvika munhu anotsvakira vana vaIsraeri zvakanaka.
\v	11	Zvino ndakasvika kuJerusarema ndokuvako mazuva matatu.
\p
\v	12	Zvino ndakasimuka usiku, ini nevarume vashoma pamwe neni; uye handina kuudza munhu izvo Mwari wangu zvaakaisa mumoyo mangu kuti ndiite paJerusarema; uye paiva pasina mhuka pamwe neni kunze kwemhuka yandaiva ndakatasva.
\v	13	Zvino ndakabuda pausiku nesuwo remupata, uye kumberi kwechitubu chebukanana, nepasuwo remufudze, ndokucherekedza masvingo eJerusarema aiva akoromoka, nemasuwo aro akapedzwa nemoto.
\v	14	Zvino ndakapfuurira kusuwo retsime, nekudziva ramambo; asi paiva pasina nzvimbo yemhuka kupfuura nepasi pangu.
\v	15	Zvino ndakakwira neparukova pausiku ndichicherekedza rusvingo; ndokutendeuka ndokupinda nepasuwo remupata, ndokudzoka.
\v	16	Zvino vatongi havana kuziva kwandaiva ndaenda uye kuti ndakaitei; uye kuvaJudha nekuvapristi nekuvabati nekuvatungamiriri nekune vamwe vaiita basa, kusvika nhasi handina ku zvitaura.
\p
\v	17	Zvino ndakati kwavari: Munoona dambudziko ratiri mariri, kuti Jerusarema idongo, nemasuwo aro akapiswa nemoto; uyai, uye ngativake rusvingo rweJerusarema, kuti tisachivazve chiseko.
\v	18	Zvino ndakavaudza zveruoko rwaMwari wangu rwakanaka pamusoro pangu, uyewo mashoko amambo aakataura kwandiri. Zvino vakati: Ngatisimuke tivake. Naizvozvo vakasimbisa maoko avo pane zvakanaka.
\p
\v	19	Asi Sanibharati muHoroni, naTobhiya muranda muAmoni, naGeshemu muArabhiya, pavakanzwa, vakatisvabaidza, ndokutizvidza, ndokuti: Chii chinhu ichi chamunoita? Munomukira mambo kanhi?
\v	20	Zvino ndakavadzosera shoko, ndokuti kwavari: Mwari wekumatenga, iye achatibudiririsa; nesu varanda vake tichasimuka tivake; asi imwi hamuna mugove nekodzero nechirangaridzo paJerusarema.
\c	3
\s	Masvingo nemasuwo eJerusarema anovakwazve
\p
\v	1	ZvinoEriashibhi mupristi mukuru wakasimuka pamwe nehama dzake vapristi, ndokuvaka suwo remakwai; ivo ndokuriita dzvene, ndokumisa magonhi aro; uye vakariita dzvene kusvika pashongwe yeMeya kusvika pashongwe yeHananeri.
\v	2	Zvinokurutivi rwake kwakavaka varume veJeriko. Nekurutivi rwavo kwakavaka Zakuri mwanakomana waImiri.
\v	3	Zvinosuwo rehove vakavaka vanakomana vaHasenaa; ivo vakamisa mapuranga aro, ndokumisa magonhi aro, kiyi dzaro, nemazariro aro.
\v	4	Nekurutivirwavo wakagadzira Meremoti mwanakomana waUriya mwanakomana waKozi. Zvino kurutivi rwavo wakagadzira Meshurami mwanakomana waBherekiya mwanakomana waMeshezabheeri. Zvino kurutivi rwavo wakagadzira Zadhoki mwanakomana waBhaana.
\v	5	Zvinokurutivi rwavo VaTekoi vakagadzira; asi vakuru vavo havana kuisa mutsipa wavo pabasa rashe wavo.
\v	6	Zvinosuwo rekare wakagadzira Joyadha mwanakomana waPaseya, naMeshurami mwanakomana waBhesodheya; ivo vakaisa mapuranga aro, ndokumisa magonhi aro, nekiyi dzaro, nemazariro aro.
\v	7	Zvinokurutivi rwavo wakagadzira Meratiya muGibhiyoni, naJadhoni muMeronoti, varume veGibhiyoni neveMizipa, kusvika kuchigaro cheushe chemutongi wemhiri kwerwizi.
\v	8	Kurutivirwake wakagadzira Uziyeri mwanakomana waHarihaya wevapfuri vegoridhe. Uye kurutivi rwake wakagadzira Hananiya, umwe wevavhenganisi vezvinonhuhwira; zvino vakagadzira Jerusarema kusvika parusvingo rwakapamhama.
\v	9	Nekurutivirwavo wakagadzira Refaya mwanakomana waHuri, mutongi wehafu yedunhu reJerusarema.
\v	10	Zvino kurutivi rwavo wakagadzira Jedhaya mwanakomana waHarumafi, uye pakatarisana neimba yake. Zvino kurutivi rwake wakagadzira Hatushi mwanakomana waHashabheneya.
\v	11	Chiyero chechipiri vakagadzira Marikia mwanakomana waHarimi, naHashubhi mwanakomana waPahati-Moabhi, neshongwe yemavira emoto.
\v	12	Zvino kurutivi rwake wakagadzira Sharumi mwanakomana waHaroheshi, mutongi wehafu yedunhu reJerusarema, iye nevakunda vake.
\v	13	Suwo remupata wakagadzira Hanuni nevagari veZanowa; ivo vakarivaka, ndokumisa magonhi aro, kiyi dzaro, nemazariro aro; uye makubhiti churu erusvingo kusvika pasuwo remufudze.
\v	14	Zvino suwo remufudze wakagadzira Marikiya mwanakomana waRekabhi, mutongi wedunhu reBheti-Hakeremi; iye wakarivaka, ndokumisa magonhi aro, kiyi dzaro, nemazariro aro.
\v	15	Zvino suwo retsime wakagadzira Sharumi mwanakomana waKori-Hoze, mutongi wedunhu reMizipa; iye wakarivaka, ndokuripfurira, ndokumisa magonhi aro, kiyi dzaro, nemazariro aro; uye rusvingo rwedziva reShera pabindu ramambo, uye kusvika pamitanho inoburuka kubva muguta raDhavhidhi.
\v	16	Shure kwake kwakagadzira Nehemiya mwanakomana waAzibhuki, mutongi wehafu yedunhu reBheti-Zuri, kusvika pakatarisana nemakuva aDhavhidhi, uye kusvika kudziva raiva raitwa, uye kusvika kuimba yevane simba.
\v	17	Shure kwake kwakagadzira vaRevhi: Rehumi mwanakomana waBhani. Kurutivi rwake wakagadzira Hashabhiya mutongi wehafu yedunhu reKeira, padunhu rake.
\v	18	Shure kwake kwakagadzira hama dzavo, Bhavhai mwanakomana waHenadhadhi, mutongi wehafu yedunhu reKeira.
\v	19	Zvino kurutivi rwake wakagadzira Ezeri mwanakomana waJeshuwa, mutongi weMizipa, chimwe chiyero, pakatarisana nemukwidza weimba yenhumbi dzehondo, pakona.
\v	20	Shure kwake kwakagadzira nekushingaira Bharuki mwanakomana waZabhai chiyero chechipiri kubva pakona kuenda kumukova weimba yaEriashibhi mupristi mukuru.
\v	21	Shure kwake kwakagadzira chimwe chiyero Meremoti mwanakomana waUriya mwanakomana waHakozi, kubva pamukova weimba yaEriashibhi kusvika pakuguma kweimba yaEriashibhi.
\v	22	Zvino shure kwake kwakagadzira vapristi, varume venzvimbo yakapoteredza.
\v	23	Shure kwake kwakagadzira Bhenjamini naHashubhi pakatarisana neimba yavo. Shure kwake kwakagadzira Azariya mwanakomana waMaaseya mwanakomana waAnania parutivi rweimba yake.
\v	24	Shure kwake kwakagadzira Bhinui mwanakomana waHenadhadhi chimwe chiyero, kubva paimba yaAzariya kusvika pakona, kunosvikazve panotenderera.
\v	25	Parari mwanakomana waUzayi, pakatarisana nekona, neshongwe inobuda paimba yepamusoro yamambo, iri pachivanze chevarindi. Shure kwake Pedhaya mwanakomana waParoshi.
\v	26	Zvino VaNetinimi vaigara muOferi, kusvika pakatarisana nesuwo remvura nechekumabvazuva neshongwe yakabudikira.
\v	27	Shure kwake VaTekoi vakagadzira chimwe chiyero pakatarisana neshongwe huru yakabudikira, kusvikawo parusvingo rweOferi.
\v	28	Kubva pamusoro pesuwo remabhiza kwakagadzira vapristi, umwe neumwe pakatarisana neimba yake.
\v	29	Shure kwavo kwakagadzira Zadhoki mwanakomana waImeri, pakatarisana neimba yake. Uye shure kwake kwakagadzira Shemaya mwanakomana waShekaniya, murindi wesuwo rekumabvazuva.
\v	30	Shure kwake kwakagadzira Hananiya mwanakomana waSheremiya, naHanuni mwanakomana wechitanhatu waZarafi chimwe chiyero. Shure kwake kwakagadzira Meshurami mwanakomana waBherekiya pakatarisana nekamuri yake.
\v	31	Shure kwake kwakagadzira Marikiya umwe wevapfuri vegoridhe, kusvika kuimba yevaNetinimi neyevatengesi, pakatarisana nesuwo raMifekadhi, kusvika pakamuri yekumusoro yepakona.
\v	32	Zvino pakati nepakati peimba yekumusoro yepakona nesuwo remakwai, vapfuri vegoridhe nevatengesi vakagadzira.
\c	4
\s	Vavengi vanoedza kuvadzivisa, asi vaJudha vanotsungirira pakuvaka Jerusarema
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika, kuti Sanibharati paakanzwa kuti tinovaka rusvingo, wakatsamwa, akava neshungu kwazvo, ndokuseka vaJudha.
\v	2	Zvinowakataura pamberi pehama dzake nehondo yeSamariya, ndokuti: VaJudha ava vasina simba vanoitei? Vachazvisimbisa here? Vachabaira here? Vachapedza pazuva  rimwe here? Vachamutsiridza mabwe here pamatutu eguruva, izvo zvakatsva here?
\v	3	ZvinoTobhiya muAmoni waiva naye, ndokuti: Neicho chavanovaka, kana gava rikakwira, richakoromora rusvingo rwavo rwemabwe.
\p
\v	4	Inzwai,Mwari wedu, nokuti tinozvidzwa; mudzosere kushora kwavo pamusoro wavo, uye muvape kuva vapambwa panyika yeutapwa.
\v	5	Zvinoregai kufukidza mhosva yavo, nechivi chavo chisadzimwa kubva pamberi penyu, nokuti vakatsamwisa pamberi pevavaki.
\v	6	Zvinotakavaka rusvingo; uye rusvingo rwose rukabatanidzwa kusvika pahafu yarwo; nokuti vanhu vaiva nemoyo wekushanda.
\p
\v	7	Asizvakaitika Sanibharati naTobhiya nevaArabhiya nevaAmoni nevaAshidhodhi pavakanzwa kuti kugadzirwa kwemasvingo eJerusarema kwakwirira, kuti maburi otanga kudzivirwa, vakatsamwa kwazvo.
\v	8	Zvinovakarangana vose pamwe chete kuti vauye varwe neJerusarema, uye kuti vaite nyonganiso mariri.
\v	9	Asitakanyengetera kuna Mwari wedu, ndokuvaisira varindi masikati neusiku, nekuda kwavo.
\v	10	Zvino Judha wakati: Simba revatakuri vemitoro raderera, uye marara akawanda; saka isu hatigoni kuvaka rusvingo.
\v	11	Zvino vavengi vedu vakati: Havangazivi kana kuona, kusvika tapinda pakati pavo ndokuvauraya, tigomisa basa.
\v	12	Zvino zvakaitika kuti vaJudha vaigara pedo navo vakasvika vakati kwatiri kagumi:  Munobva kunzvimbo dzose munofanira kudzokera kwatiri.
\p
\v	13	Naizvozvo ndakagadza panzvimbo dzakadereresa shure kwerusvingo panzvimbo dziri pachena, hongu ndakagadza vanhu zvichitevera mhuri vane minondo yavo, mapfumo avo, neuta hwavo.
\v	14	Zvino ndakatarira, ndokusimuka, ndokuti kuvakuru nekuvatongi nekune vamwe vevanhu: Musavatya; rangarirai Ishe mukuru uye anotyisa, mugorwira hama dzenyu, vanakomana venyu, nevakunda venyu, vakadzi venyu, nedzimba dzenyu.
\v	15	Zvino zvakaitika vavengi vedu pavakanzwa kuti zvazikanwa kwatiri, kuti Mwari wakakonesa kurangana kwavo, takadzokera tose kurusvingo, umwe neumwe kubasa rake.
\v	16	Zvino zvakaitika kubva pazuva iroro, hafu yevaranda vangu vakaita basa, uye hafu yavo vakabata mapfumo, nhovo, neuta, nenguvo dzematare; uye vatongi vaiva shure kweimba yose yaJudha.
\v	17	Vaivaka parusvingo, nevaitakura mitoro, vaitutira,  umwe neumwe nerumwe rwemaoko ake waishanda pabasa, nerwumwe rwakabatira pachombo chekurwa.
\v	18	Zvino vavaki, umwe neumwe waiva nemunondo wake wakasungirwa muchiuno chake vachivaka, uye wairidza hwamanda waiva parutivi rwangu.
\p
\v	19	Zvino ndakati kuvakuru nekuvatongi nekune vamwe vevanhu: Basa iguru uye rakapararira, nesu takaparadzana parusvingo, umwe ari kure neumwe.
\v	20	Panzvimbo pamunonzwa kurira kwehwamanda, unganai ipapo kwatiri. Mwari wedu achatirwira.
\v	21	Saizvozvo takashanda pabasa; uye hafu yavo vaiva vakabata mapfumo, kubva pakusimuka kwemambakwedza kusvika pakubuda kwenyeredzi.
\v	22	Panguva iyo ndakatiwo kuvanhu: Umwe neumwe nemuranda wake ngaarare mukati meJerusarema, kuti vave varindi kwatiri usiku, nevashandi masikati.
\v	23	Naizvozvo hakuna, ini, kana hama dzangu, kana varanda vangu, kana varume vevarindi vaiva shure kwangu, hakuna wedu akakurura nguvo dzedu; umwe neumwe wakadzikururira kushamba.
\c	5
\s	Nehemiya anobatsira varombo
\p
\v	1	Zvinokwakava nekuchema kukuru kwevanhu nevakadzi vavo pamusoro pehama dzavo vaJudha.
\v	2	Nokutikwakava nevakati: Vanakomana vedu nevanasikana vedu, isu takawanda, naizvozvo ngatitore zviyo kuti tidye tigorarama.
\v	3	Uyekwakava nevakati: Takaita minda yedu neminda yedu yemizambiringa nedzimba dzedu chibatiso kuti titenge zviyo panzara.
\v	4	Uyekwakava nevakati: Takakwereta mari yemutero wamambo,  paminda yedu neminda yedu yemizambiringa.
\v	5	Zvakadaroikozvino nyama yedu yakaita senyama yehama dzedu, vana vedu sevana vavo; zvino tarirai tinounza muutapwa vanakomana vedu nevakunda vedu kuva varanda; uye varipo pavakunda vedu veutapwa; uye ruoko rwedu harwuna simba, nokuti vamwe vane minda yedu neminda yedu yemizambiringa.
\p
\v	6	Zvinondakatsamwa kwazvo pandakanzwa kuchema kwavo nemashoko awa.
\v	7	Zvinomoyo wangu wakafunga mukati mangu, ndokurwisana nevakuru uye nevatongi, ndokuti kwavari: Imwi munokweretesa nechimbadzo, umwe neumwe hama yake. Zvino ndakagadza ungano huru pamusoro pavo.
\v	8	Ndokutikwavari: Isu nesimba riri matiri takatenga hama dzedu vaJudha vaiva vatengeswa kuvahedheni; nemwi muchatengesawo hama dzenyu, kana vatengeswe kwatiri here? Zvino vakanyarara, uye havana kuwana mhinduro.
\v	9	Ndikatiwo:Chinhu chamunoita hachina kunaka. Hamufaniri kufamba mukutya Mwari wedu here nekuda kwekushora kwevahedheni vavengi vedu?
\v	10	Neniwo, hama dzangu, nevaranda vangu, tanga tichikweretesa kwavari mari nezviyo. Ngatirege hedu chimbadzo ichi.
\v	11	Dzoserai henyu kwavari iko nhasi minda yavo, minda yavo yemizambiringa, minda yavo yemiorivhi, nedzimba dzavo, uye chezana chemari, nechezviyo, chewaini itsva, nechemafuta, zvamunovaripisa.
\v	12	Zvino vakati: Tichadzosa, uye hatingarevi chinhu kwavari. Tichaita saizvozvo sezvamunoreva. Zvino ndakadana vapristi, ndokuvapikisa kuti vachaita zvinoenderana neshoko iri.

\v	15	Zvino makavapa chingwa kubva kumatenga panzara yavo, ndokuvabudisira mvura kubva pabwe panyota yavo, ndokuvavimbisa kuti vachapinda kunogara nhaka yenyika yamakasimudzira ruoko rwenyu kuvapa.
\v	16	Asi ivo nemadzibaba edu vakaita nekuzvikukumadza, ndokuomesa mutsipa wavo, ndokusateerera kumirairo yenyu.
\v	17	Hongu, vakaramba kuteerera, uye havana kurangarira zvishamiso zvenyu zvamakaita kwavari, ndokuomesa mutsipa wavo, ndokugadza mutungamiriri kudzokera kuutapwa hwavo pakumukira kwavo. Asi imwi muri Mwari wekukanganwira kuzhinji, mune nyasha, netsitsi, munononoka kutsamwa, uye mune ngoni huru; uye hamuna kuvasiya.
\v	18	Uye pavaiva vazviitira mhuru yakaumbwa nekunyungundusa ndokuti: Uyu ndiMwari wenyu wakakukwidzai kubva muEgipita, vakaita kuzvidza kwakawanda kukuru;
\v	19	zvakadaro imwi mutsitsi dzenyu dzakawandisa hamuna kuvasiya murenje; shongwe yegore haina kubva pamusoro pavo pamasikati kuvatungamirira panzira, kana shongwe yemoto pausiku kuvaratidza chiedza, uye nzira yavaizofamba nayo.
\v	20	Makapawo mweya wenyu wakanaka kuvaraira, uye hamuna kunyima mana yenyu pamuromo wavo, uye makavapa mvura yenyota yavo.
\v	21	Hongu, makore makumi mana makavariritira murenje; havana kushaiwa; nguvo dzavo hadzina kusakara, uye tsoka dzavo hadzina kuzvimba.
\v	22	Makavapawo ushe hwakawanda nemarudzi, uye makavagovera kusvika kumakona, kuti vagare nhaka yenyika yaSihoni, nenyika yamambo weHeshibhoni, nenyika yaOgi mambo weBhashani.
\v	23	Zvino makawanza vana vavo senyeredzi dzekumatenga; ndokuvapinza munyika yamakavimbisa kumadzibaba avo kuti vachapinda kuno igara nhaka.
\v	24	Saizvozvo vana vakapinda ndokugara nhaka yenyika; uye imwi makawisira pasi vagari venyika iyo pamberi pavo, vaKenani; uye makavapa muruoko rwavo, pamwe nemadzimambo avo, nevanhu venyika iyo, kuti vaite navo zvichienderana nechido chavo.
\v	25	Zvino vakatapa maguta akashoshewa nenyika yakakora ndokugara nhaka yedzimba dzakazara nezvakanaka zvose, matsime akacherwa, minda yemizambiringa, neminda yemiorivhi, nemiti yakawanda yemichero; naizvozvo vakadya, uye vakaguta, ndokukora, ndokuzvifadza mukunaka kwenyu kukuru.
\v	26	Zvakadaro vakava vasingateereri, ndokukumukirai, ndokukanda murairo wenyu kumashure kwemusana wavo, ndokuuraya vaporofita venyu vaipupura vachivapikisa, kuti vavadzose kwamuri, ndokuita kuzvidza kwakawanda kukuru.
\v	27	Naizvozvo makavapa muruoko rwevavengi vavo avo vakavatambudza. Zvino munguva yedambudziko ravo vakachema kwamuri, imwi makanzwa muri kumatenga, uye zvichienderana nengoni dzenyu zhinji makavapa vaponesi, ivo ndokuvaponesa kubva muruoko rwevavengi vavo.
\v	28	Asi pavaiva vazorora, vakaitazve zvakaipa pamberi penyu; naizvozvo makavasiya muruoko rwevavengi vavo kusvika vavatonga; zvino vakadzoka vakadana kwamuri, imwi mukanzwa muri kumatenga, uye makavanunura zvichienderana nengoni dzenyu nguva zhinji.
\v	29	Zvino makavapupurira, kuti muvadzosere kumurairo wenyu, asi ivo vakaita nekuzvikukumadza, uye havana kuteerera kumirairo yenyu, uye vakatadzira mitongo yenyu (izvo munhu anozviita achararama nazvo), vakapa fudzi rinovhinyuka, uye vakaomesa mutsipa wavo, uye havana kuteerera.
\v	30	Zvakadaro makavaitira moyo murefu makore mazhinji, ndokuvapupurira nemweya wenyu neruoko rwevaporofita venyu, zvakadaro havana kupa nzeve. Naizvozvo makavapa muruoko rwevanhu venyika idzo.
\v	31	Zvakadaro netsitsi dzenyu zhinji hamuna kuvapedza, uye hamuna kuvasiya, nokuti imwi muri Mwari ane nyasha netsitsi.
\v	32	Naizvozvo ikozvino, Mwari wedu, Mwari mukuru, ane simba, uye anotyisa, anochengetedza sungano netsitsi, kutambudzika kwedu kose kwatiwana,  isu, madzimambo edu, machinda edu, nevapristi vedu, nevaporofita vedu, nemadzibaba edu, nevanhu venyu vose, kubva pamazuva emadzimambo eAsiriya kusvika nhasi, ngakurege kuita sechinhu chidiki pamberi penyu.
\v	33	Asi imwi makarurama maererano nezvose zvakatiwira, nokuti makaita chokwadi, asi isu takaita zvakaipa.
\v	34	Uye madzimambo edu, machinda edu, vapristi vedu, nemadzibaba edu, havana kuita murairo wenyu, uye havana kuteerera kumitemo yenyu, nekuzvipupuriro zvenyu zvamakavapupurira.
\v	35	Nokuti avo havana kukushumirai paushe hwavo, uye pakunaka kwenyu kukuru kwamakavapa, uye panyika yakafaranuka uye yakakora yamakapa pamberi pavo, uye havana kutendeuka pamabasa avo akaipa.
\v	36	Tarirai, tiri varanda nhasi, uye nyika yamakapa madzibaba edu, kuti vadye zvibereko zvayo nezvakanaka zvayo, tarirai, tiri varanda mairi.
\v	37	Zvino goho rayo makariwanzira madzimambo amakaisa pamusoro pedu nekuda kwezvivi zvedu; uye anotonga pamusoro pemiviri yedu nepamusoro pezvipfuwo zvedu pamadiro avo, uye tiri pakushungurudzika kukuru.
\v	38	Zvino nekuda kweizvi zvose tinoita  sungano yakasimba, uye tichiinyora; machinda edu, vaRevhi vedu, vapristi vedu ndokuisa chidhindo pamusoro  payo.
