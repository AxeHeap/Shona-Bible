\b	Ezira
\c	1
\s	Koreshi mambo wePerisiya anotendera vaJudha kudzokera kunyika yavo
\p
\v	1	Zvakaitikamugore rekutanga raKoreshi mambo wePerisiya, kuti shoko raJehovha  rakarehwa nemuromo waJeremiya ripedzisike, Jehovha wakamutsa mweya waKoreshi mambo wePerisiya kuti apfuudze kudanidzira paushe hwake hwose uyewo nechinyorwa achiti:
\p
\v	2	Ndizvozvinoreva Koreshi mambo wePerisiya: Jehovha Mwari wekumatenga wakandipa ushe hwose hwenyika, uye iye wakandiraira kumuvakira imba paJerusarema riri paJudha.
\v	3	Ndianiari pakati penyu wevanhu vake vose, Mwari wake ngaave naye, uye ngaakwire kuJerusarema riri paJudha, agovaka imba yaJehovha Mwari waIsraeri (ndiMwari) ari paJerusarema.
\v	4	Aninani wakasara panzvimbo ipi neipi paanogara semutorwa, varume venzvimbo yekwake ngavamubatsire nesirivha, uye negoridhe, uye nenhumbi, uye nemombe, pamwe nezvipo zvekuzvidira, zveimba yaMwari iri paJerusarema.
\p
\v	5	Zvinokwakasimuka misoro yemadzibaba ekwaJudha naBhenjamini, nevapristi, nevaRevhi, neumwe neumwe Mwari waaiva amutsa mweya wake, kuti vakwire kunovaka imba yaJehovha iri paJerusarema.
\v	6	Uyevose vaiva vakavapoteredza vakasimbisa maoko avo nemidziyo yesirivha, negoridhe, nenhumbi, uye nemombe, uye nezvinhu zvinokosha, kusingaverengwi zvose zvakapiwa nekuzvidira.
\p
\v	7	MamboKoreshi ndokubudisa midziyo yeimba yaJehovha, Nebhukadhinezari yaaiva abudisa paJerusarema ndokuiisa muimba yavamwari vake.
\v	8	IzvozvoKoreshi mambo wePerisiya ndokudzibudisa neruoko rwaMitiredhati mubati wehomwe, ndokudziverengera kuna Sheshibhazari muchinda waJudha.
\v	9	Zvinouhwu uwandu hwavo: Ndiro makumi matatu dzegoridhe, ndiro churu dzesirivha, mapanga makumi maviri nemapfumbamwe,
\v	10	mbiya makumi matatu dzegoridhe, dzimwe mbiya dzesirivha mazana mana negumi, imwe midziyo churu.
\v	11	Midziyo yose yegoridhe neyesirivha yaiva zvuru zvishanu nemazana mana. Zvose Sheshibhazari wakazvikwidza pamwe neavo vakakwidzwa pakutapwa vachibva kuBhabhironi kuenda kuJerusarema.
\c	10
\s	Vakadzi vatorwa vanodzingwa
\p
\v	1	ZvinoEzira wakati anyengetera, uye paaireurura, achichema nekuzviwisira pasi pamberi peimba yaMwari, kwakaungana kwaari kubva kwaIsraeri ungano huru-huru yevarume nevakadzi nevana; nokuti vanhu vakachema kuchema kukuru.
\p
\v	2	ZvinoShekaniya mwanakomana waJehieri,  umwe wevanakomana vaErami, wakapindura ndokuti kuna Ezira: Isu takatadzira Mwari wedu ndokuwana vakadzi vatorwa vendudzi dzenyika; asi ikozvino tariro iriko kwaIsraeri maererano neizvi.
\v	3	Naizvozvozvino ngatiite sungano naMwari wedu, kuti tibudise vakadzi  ava vose, nevakaberekwa navo, sezano rashe wangu, nereavo vanobvunda pamurairo waMwari wedu; uye ngazviitwe zvichienderana nemurairo.
\v	4	Simukai,nokuti nyaya iri pamusoro penyu, uye isu tinemwi; simbai muite.
\v	5	ZvinoEzira wakasimuka, ndokupikisa vakuru vevapristi, vaRevhi, naIsraeri wose, kuti vaite zvinoenderana neshoko iri. Ivo ndokupika.
\p
\v	6	ZvinoEzira wakasimuka kubva pamberi peimba yaMwari, ndokupinda mukamuri raJehohanani mwanakomana waEriashibhi; pakusvika kwakepo, chingwa haana kudya, uye mvura haana kunwa, nokuti anochema nekuda kwechitadzo chevatapwa.
\v	7	Zvinovakaita danidziro kupfuura nepaJudha neJerusarema kuvana vose veutapwa kuti vaungane paJerusarema.
\v	8	Uyeani nani asina kusvika mumazuva matatu, zvichienderana nezano remachinda nevakuru, nhumbi dzake dzose dzichatorwa, naiye achaparadzaniswa neungano yevatapwa.
\p
\v	9	Zvinovarume vose vaJudha naBhenjamini vakaungana paJerusarema mumazuva matatu; waiva mwedzi wepfumbamwe, neremakumi maviri remwedzi; vanhu vose ndokugara pachivanze cheimba yaMwari, vachibvunda nekuda kwenyaya iyi uye nekuda kwemvura huru.
\v	10	Zvino Ezira mupristi wakasimuka, ndokuti kwavari: Imwi makadarika ndokuwana vakadzi vatorwa, kuwedzera chitadzo chaIsraeri.
\v	11	Naizvozvo zvino itai reururo kuna Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba enyu, uye muite chido chake, muzvitsaure kundudzi dzenyika nekuvakadzi vatorwa.
\v	12	Zvino ungano yose yakapindura ndokuti nenzwi guru: Semashoko enyu, saizvozvo zviri pamusoro pedu kuti tizviite.
\v	13	Asi vanhu vazhinji, uye inguva yemvura zhinji, uye ha tigoni kumira kunze; uye harisi basa rezuva rimwe kana mazuva maviri, nokuti tiri vazhinji vakadarika pachinhu ichi.
\v	14	Zvino machinda edu eungano yose ngaamire, uye vose vari mumaguta edu vakawana vakadzi vatorwa ngavauye panguva dzakatarwa uye pamwe navo vakuru veguta rimwe nerimwe, nevatongi varo, kusvika tadzosa kutsamwa kunopfuta kwaMwari wedu kubva kwatiri, nekuda kwenyaya iyi.
\p
\v	15	Jonatani mwanakomana waAsaheri naJazeya mwanakomana waTikivha voga vakamira kupikisa izvi; uye Meshurami nemuRevhi Shabhetai vakavabatsira.
\v	16	Zvino vana veutapwa vakaita saizvozvo; uye Ezira mupristi, varume, misoro yemadzibaba neimba yemadzibaba avo, nevose vavo nemazita, vakatsaurwa, ndokugara pazuva rekutanga remwedzi wegumi kunzvera nyaya iyi.
\v	17	Zvino vakapedzerana nevarume vose vaiva vawana vakadzi vatorwa kusvika pazuva rekutanga remwedzi wekutanga.
\p
\v	18	Nepavanakomana vevapristi pakawanikwa vaiva vawana vakadzi vatorwa. Pavanakomana vaJeshuwa mwanakomana waJozadhaki nehama dzake: Maaseya, naEriezeri, naJaribhi, naGedhariya.
\v	19	Zvino vakapa ruoko rwavo kubudisa vakadzi vavo, nokuti vane mhosva,  vakapa gondohwe remakwai pamhosva yavo.
\v	20	Nepavanakomana vaImeri: Hanani naZebhadhia.
\v	21	Nepavanakomana vaHarimi: Maaseya, naEria, naShemaya naJehieri, naUziya.
\v	22	Nepavanakomana vaPashuri: Erioenai, Maaseya, Ishimaeri, Netaneri, Jozabhadhi, naErasha.
\v	23	NepavaRevhi: Jozabhadhi, naShimei, naKeraya (ndiye Kerita), Petahia, Judha, naEriezeri.
\v	24	Pavaimbiwo: Eriashibhi. Nepavarindi vemikova: Sharumi, naTeremi, naUri.
\v	25	Nepana Israeri: Pavanakomana vaParoshi: Ramiya, naIziya, naMarikiya, naMiyamini, naEriazari, naMarikiya, naBhenaya.
\v	26	Nepavanakomana vaErami: Mataniya, Zekariya, naJehieri, naAbhidhi, naJeremoti, naEriya.
\v	27	Nepavanakomana vaZatu: Eriowenai, Eriashibhi, Mataniya, naJeremoti, naZabhadi, naAziza.
\v	28	Nepavanakomana vaBhebhayi: Jehohanani, Hananiya, Zabhai, Atirai.
\v	29	Nepavanakomana vaBhani: Meshurami, Maruki, naAdhaya, Jashubhi, naSheari, naRamoti.
\v	30	Nepavanakomana vaPahati-Moabhi: Adhina, naKerari, Bhenaya, Maaseya, Mataniya, Bhezareeri, naBhinui, naManase.
\v	31	Nepavanakomana vaHarimi: Eriezeri, Ishiya, Marikiya, Shemaya, Shimeoni,
\v	32	Bhenjamini, Maruki, Shemariya.
\v	33	Pavanakomana vaHashumi: Matenai, Matata, Zabhadhi, Erifereti, Jeremai, Manase, Shimei.
\v	34	Pavanakomana vaBhani: Maadhai, naAmiramu, naUweri,
\v	35	Bhenaya, Bhedheya, Keruhi;
\v	36	Vhaniya, Meremoti, Eriashibhi,
\v	37	Mataniya, Matenai, naJaasa,
\v	38	naBhani, naBhinui, Shimei,
\v	39	naSheremiya, naNatani, naAdhaya,
\v	40	Makinadhebhai, Shashai, Sharai,
\v	41	Azareri, naSheremiya, Shemariya,
\v	42	Sharumi, Amariya, Josefa.
\v	43	Pavanakomana vaNebho: Jeieri, Matitia, Zabhadhi, Zebhina, Jadhai, naJoweri, Bhenaya.
\v	44	Ava vose vaiva vawana vakadzi vatorwa; uye varipo pakati pavo vakadzi vavaiva nevana navo.
\c	2
\s	Mazita evaJudha vakadzokera kunyika yavo
\p
\v	1	Zvinoava vana vedunhu vakakwira kubuda muutapwa, pane vaya vakatapwa, Nebhukadhinezari mambo weBhabhironi vaakatapira kuBhabhironi, ndokudzoka kuJerusarema naJudha, umwe neumwe kuguta rake;
\v	2	avovakauya naZerubhabheri, Jeshuwa, Nehemiya, Seraya, Reeraya, Modhekai, Bhirishani, Misipari, Bhigivhai, Rehumi, Bhaana. Uwandu hwevarume verudzi rwaIsraeri:
\v	3	VanavaParoshi, zvuru zviviri nezana nemakumi manomwe nevaviri.
\v	4	VanavaShefatiya, mazana matatu nemakumi manomwe nevaviri.
\v	5	VanavaAra, mazana manomwe nemakumi manomwe nevashanu.
\v	6	VanavaPahati-Moabhi, vevana vaJeshuwa-Joabhi, zvuru zviviri nemazana masere negumi nevaviri.
\v	7	VanavaErami, churu nemazana maviri nemakumi mashanu nevana.
\v	8	VanavaZatu, mazana mapfumbamwe nemakumi mana nevashanu.
\v	9	VanavaZakai, mazana manomwe nemakumi matanhatu.
\v	10	Vana vaBhani, mazana matanhatu nemakumi mana nevaviri.
\v	11	Vana vaBhebhayi, mazana matanhatu nemakumi maviri nevatatu.
\v	12	Vana vaAzagadhi, churu nemazana maviri nemakumi maviri nevaviri.
\v	13	Vana vaAdhonikami, mazana matanhatu nemakumi matanhatu nevatanhatu.
\v	14	Vana vaBhigivhai, zvuru zviviri nemakumi mashanu nevatanhatu.
\v	15	Vana vaAdhini, mazana mana nemakumi mashanu nevana.
\v	16	Vana vaAteri waHezekia, makumi mapfumbamwe nevasere.
\v	17	Vana vaBhezai, mazana matatu nemakumi maviri nevatatu.
\v	18	Vana vaJora, zana negumi nevaviri.
\v	19	Vana vaHashumi, mazana maviri nemakumi maviri nevatatu.
\v	20	Vana veGibhari, makumi mapfumbamwe nevashanu.
\v	21	Vana veBheterehemu, zana nemakumi maviri nevatatu.
\v	22	Varume veNetofa, makumi mashanu nevatanhatu.
\v	23	Varume veAnatoti, zana nemakumi maviri nevasere.
\v	24	Vana veAzimavheti, makumi mana nevaviri.
\v	25	Vana veKiriati-Arimi, Kefira, neBheeroti, mazana manomwe nemakumi mana nevatatu.
\v	26	Vana veRama neGebha, mazana matanhatu nemakumi maviri neumwe.
\v	27	Varume veMikimashi, zana nemakumi maviri nevaviri.
\v	28	Varume veBheteri neAi, mazana maviri nemakumi maviri nevatatu.
\v	29	Vana veNebho, makumi mashanu nevaviri.
\v	30	Vana veMagibhishi, zana nemakumi mashanu nevatanhatu.
\v	31	Vana veumwe Erami, churu nemazana maviri nemakumi mashanu nevana.
\v	32	Vana vaHarimi, mazana matatu nemakumi maviri.
\v	33	Vana veRodhi, Hadhidhi, neOno, mazana manomwe nemakumi maviri nevashanu.
\v	34	Vana veJeriko, mazana matatu nemakumi mana nevashanu.
\v	35	Vana veSenaha, zvuru zvitatu nemazana matanhatu nemakumi matatu.
\p
\v	36	Vapristi: Vana vaJedhaya, veimba yaJeshuwa, mazana mapfumbamwe nemakumi manomwe nevatatu.
\v	37	Vana veImeri, churu nemakumi mashanu nevaviri.
\v	38	Vana vaPashuri, churu nemazana maviri nemakumi mana nevanomwe.
\v	39	Vana vaHarimi, churu negumi nevanomwe.
\p
\v	40	VaRevhi: Vana vaJeshuwa naKadhimieri, vevana vaHodhavhiya, makumi manomwe nevana.
\p
\v	41	Vaimbi: Vana vaAsafi, zana nemakumi maviri nevasere.
\p
\v	42	Vana vevarindi vemikova: Vana vaSharumi, vana vaAteri, vana vaTarimoni, vana vaAkubhi, vana vaHatita, vana vaShobhai, vose zana nemakumi matatu nevapfumbamwe.
\p
\v	43	VaNetinimi: Vana vaZiha, vana vaHasufa, vana vaTabhaoti,
\v	44	vana vaKerosi, vana vaSiaha, vana vaPadhoni,
\v	45	vana vaRebhana, vana vaHagabha, vana vaAkubhi,
\v	46	vana vaHagabhi, vana vaShamirai, vana vaHanani,
\v	47	vana vaGidheri, vana vaGahari, vana vaReaya,
\v	48	vana vaRezini, vana vaNekodha, vana vaGazami,
\v	49	vana vaUza, vana vaPasea, vana vaBhesayi,
\v	50	vana vaAsina, vana vaMeunimi, vana vaNefusimi,
\v	51	vana vaBhakibhuki, vana vaHakufa, vana vaHarihuri,
\v	52	vana vaBhaziruti, vana vaMehidha, vana vaHarisha,
\v	53	vana vaBharikosi, vana vaSisera, vana vaTama,
\v	54	vana vaNeziya, vana vaHatifa.
\p
\v	55	Vana vevaranda vaSoromoni: Vana vaSotai, vana vaHasofereti, vana vaPerudha,
\v	56	vana vaJaara, vana vaDharikoni, vana vaGidheri,
\v	57	vana vaShefatia, vana vaHatiri, vana vaPokereti-Hazebhaimi, vana vaAmi.
\v	58	VaNetinimi vose nevana vevaranda vaSoromoni vaiva mazana matatu nemakumi mapfumbamwe nevaviri.
\p
\v	59	Zvino ava ndeavo vakakwira kubva paTeri-Mera, Teri-Harisha, Kerubhi, Adhani, Imeri; asi vaiva vasingagoni kududza imba yemadzibaba avo, nembeu yavo, kana vaibva kwaIsraeri.
\v	60	Vana vaDheraya, vana vaTobhiya, vana vaNekodha, mazana matanhatu nemakumi mashanu nevaviri.
\p
\v	61	Nepavana vevapristi: Vana vaHabhaya, vana vaHakozi, vana vaBharizirai, wakatora mukadzi kuvakunda vaBharizirai muGiriyadhi, ndokutumidzwa nezita ravo.
\v	62	Ava vakatsvaka kunyorwa kwavo  pane avo vakaverengwa nemarudzi, asi havana kuwanikwa; ndokubviswa paupristi sevakasviba.
\v	63	Mutongi ndokureva kwavari kuti varege kudya pazvinhu zvitsvene-tsvene, kusvika kwamuka mupristi ane Urimi neTumimi.
\p
\v	64	Ungano yose pamwe yaiva zvuru makumi mana nezviviri nemazana matatu nemakumi matanhatu,
\v	65	vasingaverengi varanda vavo nevarandakadzi vavo; avo vaiva zvuru zvinomwe nemazana matatu nemakumi matatu nevanomwe; uye vaiva nevaimbi varume nevaimbi vechikadzi mazana maviri.
\v	66	Mabhiza avo aiva mazana manomwe nemakumi matatu nematanhatu; mahesera avo, mazana maviri nemakumi mana nemashanu;
\v	67	makamera avo, mazana mana nemakumi matatu nemashanu; mbongoro, zvuru zvitanhatu nemazana manomwe nemakumi maviri.
\p
\v	68	Ne vamwe vemisoro yemadzibaba pakupinda kwavo muimba yaJehovha iri muJerusarema vakapa kuimba yaMwari nechido kuimisa panzvimbo yayo.
\v	69	Vakapa zvichienderana nekugona kwavo kuhomwe yebasa, ngoda dzegoridhe zvuru makumi matanhatu neimwe, nepondo dzesirivha zvuru zvishanu, nenguvo refu dzevapristi zana.
\v	70	Zvino vapristi nevaRevhi ne vamwe vevanhu nevaimbi nevarindi vemikova nevaNetinimi vakagara mumaguta avo, naIsraeri wose mumaguta avo.
\c	3
\s	Vanotanga kuvaka tembere paJerusarema
\p
\v	1	Mwedziwechinomwe wakati wasvika, uye vana vaIsraeri vava mumaguta, vanhu vakaungana semunhu umwe paJerusarema.
\v	2	Jeshuwamwanakomana waJozadhaki ndokusimuka, nehama dzake vapristi, naZerubhabheri mwanakomana waShearitieri, nehama dzake, ndokuvaka aritari yaMwari waIsraeri kuti vape pairi zvipo zvinopiswa, sezvakanyorwa pamurairo waMozisi munhu waMwari.
\v	3	Zvinovakagadzika aritari panheyo dzayo, nokuti kutya kwaiva pamusoro pavo nekuda kwevanhu venyika idzo; zvino vakapa zvipo zvinopiswa kuna Jehovha pamusoro payo, zvipo zvinopiswa zvemangwanani nezvemadekwani.
\v	4	Vakaitawomutambo wematumba sezvakanyorwa; uye  vakapa zvipo zvinopiswa zuva nezuva neuwandu, setsika, nyaya yezuva nezuva rayo.
\v	5	Uyepashure chipo chinopiswa rinhi narinhi, zvose nezvekugara kwemwedzi nezvemitambo yose yakatarwa yaJehovha yaiva yaitwa mitsvene, uye zveumwe neumwe wakapa nekuzvidira chipo chekuzvidira kuna Jehovha.
\v	6	Kubvapazuva rekutanga remwedzi wechinomwe vakatanga kupa zvipo zvinopiswa kuna Jehovha. Asi tembere yaJehovha yaiva isina kuteyiwa.
\v	7	Vakapawomari kuvavezi vemabwe nekuvavezi vematanda, uye zvekudya nezvekunwa nemafuta  vakapa kuvaSidhoni nekuvaTire, kuti vauye nemiti yemisidhari kubva kuRebhanoni kuuya pagungwa reJopa, zvichienderana netendero yaKoreshi mambo wePerisiya maererano navo.
\p
\v	8	Zvinomugore rechipiri rekusvika kwavo paimba yaMwari paJerusarema, mumwedzi wechipiri, Zerubhabheri mwanakomana waShearitieri, naJeshuwa mwanakomana waJozadhaki, nevakasara vehama dzavo vapristi nevaRevhi, nevose vaiva vauya kuJerusarema vachibva muutapwa vakatanga, ndokumisa vaRevhi kubva ane makore makumi maviri zvichikwira kutungamirira basa reimba yaJehovha.
\v	9	Zvinokwakamira Jeshuwa, vanakomana vake, nehama dzake, Kadhimieri nevanakomana vake, vanakomana vaJudha, se munhu umwe, kutungamirira vaiti vebasa muimba yaMwari: Vanakomana vaHenadhadhi, vanakomana vavo, nehama dzavo vaRevhi.
\v	10	Vavaki vakati vateya nheyo dzetembere yaJehovha, vakamisa vapristi vakapfeka, vane hwamanda, nevaRevhi vanakomana vaAsafi vane makandira, kuti varumbidze Jehovha, zvichitevera maoko aDhavhidhi mambo waIsraeri.
\v	11	Zvino vakaimba pamwe nemapoka pakurumbidza nekupa kuvonga kuna Jehovha  vachiti: Nokuti wakanaka, nokuti ngoni dzake dziripo nekusingaperi kuna Israeri. Vanhu vose ndokudanidzira nekudanidzira kukuru pakurumbidza Jehovha; nokuti nheyo dzeimba yaJehovha dzaiva dzateyiwa.
\v	12	Asi vazhinji vevapristi nevaRevhi, nevakuru vemadzibaba, nevatana vaiva vaona imba yekutanga, nheyo dzeimba iyi padzaiva dzateyiwa pamberi pemeso avo, vakachema misodzi neinzwi guru; asi vazhinji vakasimudza inzwi mukudanidzira nemufaro;
\v	13	zvekuti vanhu vakatadza kuziva inzwi rekudanidzira kubva painzwi rekuchema kwevanhu; nokuti vanhu vakadanidzira nekudanidzira kwemufaro mukuru; inzwi ndokunzwikwa kusvika kure-kure.
\c	4
\s	VaSamariya vanoedza kuvadzivisa kuvaka tembere
\p
\v	1	VavengivaJudha naBhenjamini pavakanzwa kuti vana veutapwa vavakira Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri tembere,
\v	2	ndokuswederakuna Zerubhabheri, nekuvakuru vemadzibaba, ndokuti kwavari: Ngativake nemwi, nokuti tinotsvaka Mwari wenyu semwi; uye tinomubaira kubva pamazuva aEsari-Hadhoni mambo weAsiriya wakatikwidza pano.
\v	3	AsiZerubhabheri naJeshuwa nevamwe vevakuru vemadzibaba aIsraeri vakati kwavari: Hazvisi zvenyu nesu kuvakira Mwari wedu imba; asi tisu toga tichavakira Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri, mambo Koreshi mambo wePerisiya sezvaakatiraira.
\v	4	Zvinozvakaitika vanhu venyika iyo vashaisa simba maoko evanhu vaJudha, ndokuvatambudza pakuvaka.
\v	5	Zvinovakavahaira varairiri, kunyonganisa chinangwa chavo mazuva ose aKoreshi mambo wePerisiya, uye kusvika pakutonga kwaDhariyasi mambo wePerisiya.
\v	6	NepakutongakwaAhasuwerusi, pakutanga kwekutonga kwake, vakanyora mhosva pamusoro pevagari vekwaJudha neJerusarema.
\p
\v	7	Zvinomumazuva aAritakiserikisi, Bhishirami, Mitiredhati, Tabheeri, nevamwe vevafambidzani vake vakanyorera kuna Aritakiserikisi mambo wePerisiya; uye chinyorwa chetsamba chaiva chakanyorwa nechiAramiya, nekududzirwa nechiAramiya.
\v	8	Rehumimurairiri naShimishai munyori vakanyora tsamba maererano neJerusarema kuna Aritakiserikisi mambo nenzira iyi:
\v	9	IpapoRehumi murairiri, naShimishai munyori, nevamwe vevafambidzani vake, VaDhinai, nevaAfarisatiki, VaTariperi, VaAfarisi, VaArikevhi, VaBhabhironi, VaShushaniki, VaDhehai, VaErami,

\v	12	Asi shure kweizvozvo madzibaba edu akatsamwisa Mwari wekumatenga, iye akavapa muruoko rwaNebhukadhenezari mambo weBhabhironi, muKaradhiya; uyo wakaparadza imba ino, ndokutapira vanhu kuBhabhironi.
\v	13	Asi mugore rekutanga raKoreshi mambo weBhabhironi, mambo Koreshi wakamisa murairo wekuvaka imba iyi yaMwari.
\v	14	Nemidziyowo yegoridhe nesirivha yeimba yaMwari, Nebhukadhinezari yaakatora kubva mutembere yaiva paJerusarema, ndokuuya nayo kutembere yeBhabhironi, iyo mambo Koreshi wakabudisa mutembere yeBhabhironi, ndokupiwa wainzi Sheshibhazari, waakaita mutongi.
\v	15	Zvino wakati kwaari: Tora midziyo iyi, enda ugonoiburusira mutembere iri paJerusarema, uye imba yaMwari ngaivakwe panzvimbo yayo.
\v	16	Ipapo uyu Sheshibhazari wakauya, akateya nheyo dzeimba yaMwari iri paJerusarema; zvino kubva panguva iyo kusvika ikozvino yaivakwa, asi haina kupera.
\v	17	Naizvozvo zvino, kana zvakanaka kuna mambo, ngavanzvere muimba yefuma yamambo iripo paBhabhironi, kana zviri  zvakadaro, kuti mutemo wakaitwa namambo Koreshi kuvaka imba ino yaMwari paJerusarema, mambo agotuma kwatiri kuda kwake pamusoro pechinhu ichi.

\p
\v	3	Mugorerekutanga raKoreshi mambo, Koreshi mambo wakaita murairo: Imba yaMwari paJerusarema, imba ngaivakwe, nzvimbo pavanopa zvibairo, uye nheyo dzayo ngadziteiwe; urefu hwayo makubhiti makumi matanhatu, upamhi hwayo makubhiti makumi matanhatu;

\v	1	Zvinoshure kwezvinhu izvi, pakutonga kwaAritakiserikisi mambo wePerisiya: Ezira mwanakomana waSeraya, mwanakomana waAzariya, mwanakomana waHirikiya,

\v	9	Nokutipane rekutanga remwedzi wekutanga wakatanga kukwira achibva kuBhabhironi, uye nerekutanga remwedzi weshanu wakasvika paJerusarema, zvichienderana neruoko rwakanaka rwaMwari wake pamusoro pake.

\p
\v	12	Aritakiserikisi mambo wemadzimambo, kuna Ezira mupristi, munyori wemurairo waMwari wekumatenga,  rugare rwakakwana, uye panguva yakadai.

\v	17	kuti utenge nekukurumidza nemari iyi nzombe diki, makondohwe, makwayana, pamwe nezvipo zvazvo zvechidyiwa nezvipo zvazvo zvechinwiwa, ndokuzvipa pamusoro pearitari yeimba yaMwari wenyu iri paJerusarema.

\v	20	Nezvimwe zvinoshaikwa paimba yaMwari wako, zvaunofanira kuripa, ripa kubva paimba yefuma yamambo.
\v	21	Zvino ini mambo Aritakiserikisi, mutemo unomiswa neni kuvabati vose vehomwe vari mhiri kwerwizi, kuti zvose Ezira mupristi, munyori wemurairo waMwari wekumatenga zvaanokumbira kwamuri zviitwe nekukurumidza,
\v	22	kusvika pamatarenda zana esirivha, uye pazviyero zana zvezviyo, nepamabhati zana ewaini, nepamabhati zana emafuta, nemunyu usinganyoreki.

\v	26	Zvino ani nani asingaiti murairo waMwari wako, nemurairo wamambo, mutongo ngaukurumidze kuitwa kwaari, ungava werufu, kana kudzingwa, kana kutorerwa nhumbi, kana kuva muusungwa.

\v	26	Ndakayererawo muruoko rwavo matarenda mazana matanhatu nemakumi mashanu esirivha, nematarenda zana emidziyo yesirivha,  nematarenda zana egoridhe;
\v	27	nembiya makumi maviri dzegoridhe rakasvika madhariki churu; nemidziyo miviri yendarira yakanaka inopenya yaishuviwa segoridhe.
\v	28	Zvino ndakati kwavari: Imwi muri vatsvene kuna Jehovha, nemidziyo mitsvene, uye sirivha negoridhe, chipo chekuzvidira kuna Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba enyu.
\v	29	Mu zvirindire, uye muchengetedze, kusvika mazviyera pamberi pemisoro yevapristi nevaRevhi, nemisoro yemadzibaba aIsraeri paJerusarema,  mumakamuri eimba yaJehovha.
\v	30	Zvino vapristi nevaRevhi vakagamuchira chiyero chesirivha nechegoridhe nechemidziyo, kuti vazviise paJerusarema paimba yaMwari wedu.
\p
\v	31	Zvino takabva parwizi rweAhavha neregumi nemaviri remwedzi wekutanga kuenda kuJerusarema; uye ruoko rwaMwari wedu rwaiva pamusoro pedu, akatinunura paruoko rwemuvengi nemuvandiri panzira.
\v	32	Zvino takasvika paJerusarema, ndokugarapo mazuva matatu.
\p
\v	33	Zvino pazuva rechina sirivha negoridhe nemidziyo zvakayerwa muimba yaMwari wedu neruoko rwaMeremoti mwanakomana wemupristi Uriya; uye pamwe naye Eriazari mwanakomana waPinehasi, uye pamwe navo Jozabhadhi mwanakomana waJeshuwa, naNowadhiya mwanakomana waBhinui, vaRevhi,
\v	34	pauwandu  uye pauremu hwezvose; uye uremu hwose hwakanyorwa panguva iyoyo.
\v	35	Vana veutapwa vaiva vadzoka pautapwa vakapa zvipo zvinopiswa kuna Mwari waIsraeri, nzombe diki gumi nembiri dzaIsraeri wose, makondohwe makumi mapfumbamwe nematanhatu, makwayana makumi manomwe nemanomwe, nhongo gumi nembiri dzembudzi, chipo chechivi; zvose chiri chipo chinopiswa kuna Jehovha.
\p
\v	36	Zvino vakapa mirairo yamambo kumachinda amambo nevatongi mhiri kwerwizi, ivo ndokutsigira vanhu neimba yaMwari.
\c	9
\s	Ezira anochema pamusoro pezvivi zvevanhu vake
\p
\v	1	Zvinopakupera kwezvinhu izvi kwakaswedera kwandiri machinda achiti: Vanhu vaIsraeri nevapristi nevaRevhi havana kuzvitsaura kubva pavanhu vedzinyika zvichienderana nezvinyangadzo zvavo, ivo vaKenani, vaHiti, vaPerezi, vaJebhusi, vaAmoni, vaMoabhu, vaEgipita, nevaAmori.
\v	2	Nokutivakazvitorera kubva pavakunda vavo ivo nevanakomana vavo, kusvika vasanganisa mbeu tsvene nemarudzi edzinyika, hongu, ruoko rwemachinda nevabati rwaiva rwekutanga pachitadzo ichi.
\p
\v	3	Zvinopakunzwa kwangu chinhu ichi, ndakabvarura nguvo yangu nejazi rangu, ndokudzura pavhudzi remusoro wangu nerendebvu dzangu, ndokugara pasi ndakakanuka.
\v	4	Zvinokwakaungana kwandiri umwe neumwe waibvunda pamashoko aMwari waIsraeri nekuda kwechitadzo chevatapwa; zvino ndakagara ndakakanuka kusvika pachipo chemadekwani.
\p
\v	5	Zvinopachipo chemadekwani ndakasimuka pakuninipiswa kwangu, uye pakubvarura kwangu nguvo yangu nejazi rangu; ndikakotamira pasi nemabvi angu, ndikatandavadzira maoko angu kuna Jehovha Mwari wangu;
\v	6	ndokuti:Mwari wangu, ndinonyara uye ndinosvoda kusimudzira chiso changu kwamuri, Mwari wangu; nokuti zvivi zvedu zvawanda kupfuura misoro  yedu, uye mhosva yedu yakakura kusvika kumatenga.
\v	7	Kubvapamazuva emadzibaba edu tiri muchitadzo chikuru kusvika pazuva rino; zvino nekuda kwekuipa kwedu isu, madzimambo edu, vapristi vedu, takapiwa muruoko rwemadzimambo edzinyika, kumunondo, mukutapwa, nemukupambiwa, uye munyadzi dzechiso, sezuva rino.
\v	8	Zvinoikozvino kwechinguvana tsitsi dzaitwa naJehovha Mwari wedu, kutisiira kupukunyuka, nekutipa mbambo panzvimbo yake tsvene, kuti Mwari wedu avhenekere meso edu, uye atipe raramo diki pautapwa hwedu.
\v	9	Nokutitiri nhapwa; asi Mwari wedu haana kutisiya muutapwa hwedu, asi wakapfuudzira tsitsi kwatiri pamberi pemadzimambo ePerisiya, kutipa raramo, kusimudza imba yaMwari wedu, nekugadzirisa matongo ayo, nekutipa rusvingo paJudha nepaJerusarema.
\v	10	Zvino ikozvino, Mwari wedu, tichatii shure kweizvi? Nokuti takasiya mirairo yenyu,
\v	11	yamakaraira neruoko rwevaranda venyu vaporofita, muchiti: Nyika kwamunoenda kuigara nhaka inyika yakasviba nekusviba kwemarudzi enyika idzo, nezvinyangadzo zvavo, zvakaizadza kubva kumucheto kusvika kumucheto, nekusachena kwadzo.
\v	12	Naizvozvo ikozvino, regai kupa vakunda venyu kuvanakomana vavo, kana kutorera vakunda vavo vanakomana venyu, kana kutsvaka rugare rwavo kana zvakanaka zvavo kusvika rinhi narinhi, kuti musimbe mudye zvakanaka zvenyika, muisiire vana venyu ive nhaka kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	13	Zvino shure kwezvose zvakatiwira, nekuda kwezviito zvedu zvakaipa uye kwemhosva yedu yakakura, nokuti imwi Mwari wedu makazvidzora  pakutiranga zvishoma pane zvivi zvedu, ndokutipa kupukunyuka kwakadai.
\v	14	Tichagurazve mirairo yenyu here tigowanana nerudzi rwezvinyangadzo izvi? Hamungatitsamwiri kusvika ma tiparadza here, kusvika kusina musiiwa kana kupukunyuka?
\v	15	Jehovha, Mwari waIsraeri, imwi makarurama; nokuti tasiirwa kupukunyuka, sezuva rino; tarirai tiri pamberi penyu pamhosva yedu; nokuti hakuna angamira pamberi penyu nekuda kwechinhu ichi.
